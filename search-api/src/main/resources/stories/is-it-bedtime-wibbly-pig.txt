Is it Bedtime, Wibbly Pig?
This story is in a question and answer format. 
The narrator asks Wibbly Pig if it is bedtime already, and each time, Wibbly Pig gives amusing excuses to avoid going to bed. 
Wibbly Pig is taking a shower when the narrator asks if it is bedtime. 
Wibbly Pig replies that it is not, since she is still in the bath. 
When asked if she has finished the bath, she says she is still drying her toes. 
The story goes on, with Wibbly Pig coming up with various excuses to avoid bedtime. 
The story ends when Wibbly finally falls asleep with her cuddly toys, Dimple the teddy bear, Pigley the pig, and Flop, the bunny.
